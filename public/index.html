About us

Often what is taught in schools and colleges is not exactly what is required in the Industry.

This creates a gap between what is taught and what is required called as the ‘Skill Gap’.

AVATAR is a Digital Academy established to solve this problem.

Our Vision

To fill the skill gap in India.

Our Mission

To provide world-class training to learners, creating a new generation of skilled personnel capable of working at a global level making India proud.
